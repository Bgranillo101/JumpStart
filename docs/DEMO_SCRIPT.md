# JumpStart Demo Script

A step-by-step walkthrough for the recorded demonstration video.

---

## Setup (before recording)
- Clear browser cookies / use Incognito
- Backend running at `http://localhost:8080` (or Render URL)
- Frontend running at `http://localhost:5173/JumpStart/`

---

## Scene 1 — Landing Page (~30 s)

1. Open the app at `http://localhost:5173/JumpStart/`.
2. Point out the animated force-directed org chart in the hero.
3. Mention the value proposition: "JumpStart helps startup founders build the right team with AI-powered role assignment."
4. Click **Get Started**.

---

## Scene 2 — Registration & Onboarding (~60 s)

1. On the path selector, choose **Create a Company**.
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Username: `demo_founder`
   - Email: `demo@jumpstart.dev`
   - Password: `demo1234`
   - Company / Startup Name: `NeuroFlow`
   - Full Name: `Alex Rivera`
   - Role / Title: `CEO & Co-founder`
   - Skills: type `Product Strategy`, press Enter → type `Fundraising`, press Enter
3. Click **Create My Profile**.

---

## Scene 3 — Dashboard Overview (~60 s)

1. Land on the Dashboard — point out the greeting and team overview card.
2. Show the Team Overview card with the startup name and description.
3. Note the Skill Heatmap placeholder: "We haven't run analysis yet — let's invite a teammate first."

---

## Scene 4 — Invite a Teammate (~30 s)

1. Click the **Team** tab in the sidebar.
2. Click **Generate Invite Link** — copy the link.
3. Open a new Incognito tab, paste the link, register as a second user:
   - Username: `dev_partner`
   - Email: `dev@jumpstart.dev`
   - Password: `demo1234`
   - Skills: `React`, `TypeScript`, `Node.js`
4. Show that `dev_partner` now appears in the Team Members list.

---

## Scene 5 — AI Analysis (~90 s)

1. Switch back to the first account's dashboard.
2. Navigate to the **Analysis** tab.
3. Click **Run AI Analysis** — wait for results.
4. Walk through the results:
   - **Role Assignments** — each member's AI-recommended role, confidence score, and reasoning.
   - **Role Gaps** — critical roles the team is missing and what skills to hire for.
   - **Skill Heatmap** — radar chart showing team coverage across six categories.
   - **Team Readiness Score** — overall score 0–100.

---

## Scene 6 — Profile Page (~30 s)

1. Click the user avatar in the sidebar bottom → navigates to `/profile`.
2. Click **Edit Profile**.
3. Update headline: "Building the future of team intelligence."
4. Drag in a profile photo.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

---

## Scene 7 — Wrap-up (~15 s)

1. Return to Dashboard — sidebar now shows the updated name and role.
2. Mention the live URL: `bgranillo101.github.io/JumpStart`.
3. End recording.
